The brief

Crystal Tours and Travel is a leading travel agency based in Nairobi Kenya that specializes in both local and International Travel. To continue offering seamless experiences for their customers, they wanted to include a Hotel booking feature within their site to compliment the Tour booking services and Flight booking services that were already in place.

Our approach

Keeping in mind that Crystal tours & travel was catering to a global market, our goal was a service that transcended geography and could provide customers with access to hotels worldwide. This led us to opting for the TBO hotel booking api which we integrated with the Crystal Tours & Travel site. However, Crystal Tours & Travel had to register as a TBO agency in order to have access to hotel booking api for integration to their site.

The result

Crystal Tours & Travel clients are now able to make direct hotel booking, globally. Their clients are able to book a flight, secure a tour or book a hotel, all within the same website.
