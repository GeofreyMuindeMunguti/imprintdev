The brief

Digitising processes in today’s business landscape is proving to be one of the leading ways businesses can remain competitive by improving their process efficiencies as well as tapping into the opportunities presented by the growing digital economy.

Access to finance or working credit also continues to be a lifeline for many small businesses. For these businesses, digitization is becoming critical in order to demonstrate traction and increase chances of access to finance by lenders.

Our approach

We built a multi-service Enterprise Resource Planner that digitises general operational workflows for SMEs. The ERP is a web based system with customizable modules based on a business’s needs and a price point tailored specifically for small businesses.

The result

For clients like Crystal Tours, the ERP allows for optimisation of their business processes through timely and accurate access to data/information, giving crystal Tours a much needed edge in today’s competitive business landscape.
