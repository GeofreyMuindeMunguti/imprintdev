The brief

Joanna K is a leading Kenyan cosmetics brand. Founder and Entrepreneur, Joanna K, had created a line of beauty products that she needed to make available to her fans and customers. She quickly realized that a brick and mortar approach would limit product access to her customers across the country.

Our approach

We created an online e-commerce website for Joanna K products that allowed customers to place their orders, track their goods as they were being delivered and make payments online.

The result

An easy-to-navigate and user friendly E-commerce system through which her products could be ordered, deliveries tracked and payments received.
