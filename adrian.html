The brief

Adrian Kenya is a technology solutions provider that specializes in the deployment and maintenance of infrastructural solutions in Telecommunications, Power, IT, and Oil & Gas. Their procurement team had become aware of the limitations of a manual approach to fuelling their fleet of business vehicles. Their manual approach was slowing down their operations which in turn threatened to interfere with their quality of service to their customers.

Our approach

We integrated their processes into an Odoo fleet management system to create a mobile application through which drivers could easily make their fuel requests, have managers review these requests and make payments for the same. The platform would also generate regular reports on the fuel consumption and finance allocation to track and optimize their business’s budget allocations for fuel.

The result

A drivers mobile application through which Adrian drivers make fuel requests and upload any required documents. The app is also used by the admin team to receive instant notifications on fuelling requests made, view requests and approve the requests for fuelling.
