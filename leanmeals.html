The brief

Lean Meals is a Nutrition Consultancy that promotes good food habits. Their founder and Nutritionist, Rachel, approached us to help create a meal plan app whose purpose was to guide users on how to make healthier food choices. She had come to identify the high consultation fees in the nutrition field as a barrier to many people who were interested in learning how to eat better and wanted to create an easily accessible tool that could provide users with meal recommendations tailored to their health/body goals.

The result

A user friendly meal plan app that runs on both iOS and Android platforms.
